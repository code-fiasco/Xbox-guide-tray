HidHide (optional)
------------------
Copyright (c) Nefarius Software Solutions e.U.
Licensed under the MIT License.
https://github.com/nefarius/HidHide

HidHide is a kernel-mode filter driver used to isolate controller input
during the power menu. It is installed only when you select that option
in the Xbox Guide Tray setup wizard.
